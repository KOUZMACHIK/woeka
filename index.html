About MEDI

International Conference on Model and Data Engineering (MEDI)

The 15th International Conference on Model and Data Engineering (MEDI 2026) will take place at Neapolis University Pafos in Pafos, Cyprus. Cyprus is a place where centuries-old history blends seamlessly with contemporary advancements. MEDI 2026 provides a special chance to engage with state-of-the-art research in model and data engineering while experiencing the island's rich cultural and historical backdrop. The conference seeks to unite researchers, professionals, and industry experts from across the globe to present new discoveries, examine emerging developments, and encourage collaboration in the areas of model and data engineering. We welcome submissions of high-quality research papers, industry case studies, and forward-looking contributions that advance both the theoretical and practical aspects of these domains.

The conference proceedings will be published as a dedicated volume in the Springer Lecture Notes in Business Information Processing (LNBIP) series. This internationally recognized series provides a high-quality forum for the dissemination of original research, innovative methodologies, and advanced applications in the field of business information systems. Inclusion in the LNBIP series ensures broad visibility within the academic and professional communities, as all accepted papers will undergo thorough editorial processing and will be made available through Springer's digital library. As a result, contributors will benefit from enhanced global reach, long-term archival, and indexing in major scholarly databases.
